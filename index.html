Short Bio

Minrui Xu is pursuing his Ph.D. degree in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Dusit Niyato, Fellow of IEEE. Before that, in 2021, he received B.S. degree from Sun Yat-sen University (中山大学), Guangzhou, China, supervised by Prof. Wuhui Chen in InplusLab.

His research interest broadly lies in the areas of Metaverse, mobile edge/quantum computing, deep reinforcement learning and incentive mechanism. He initiated several new fields such as edge-enabled Metaverse, quantum-secured space-air-ground integrated networks, and mobile quantum computing.
